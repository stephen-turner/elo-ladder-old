Rules

  1. You may play a match to any length n. The first player to reach that score wins. Only winning or losing counts; the exact score doesn't matter.
  2. The Crawford rule is in effect: if one player reaches (n - 1) points, the other player may not double in the next game, but may double at any time in subsequent games.
